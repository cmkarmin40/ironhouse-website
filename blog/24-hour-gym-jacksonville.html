Best 24 Hour Gyms in Jacksonville FL [2026]

Train on your schedule. Here are Jacksonville's best gyms with 24/7 access.

Jacksonville runs on shift work. Between the Navy bases, hospitals, fire departments, warehouses, and everything else that keeps this city running around the clock, a lot of people in Duval don't work a standard 9-to-5. If you're getting off at midnight or waking up at 4 AM, you need a gym that's open when you need it — not just during "business hours."

Here's a breakdown of the best 24 hour gyms in Jacksonville FL, what each one offers, and how to choose the right one for your training style and schedule.

Why 24/7 Gym Access Matters

Having round-the-clock access to your gym isn't just about convenience — it's about consistency. Consistency is the single most important factor in getting results, whether your goal is strength, muscle, fat loss, or general health. If your gym is only open from 5 AM to 10 PM, and your shift ends at 11 PM, you're going to miss workouts. Missed workouts become missed weeks. Missed weeks become lost progress.

24/7 access also means you can train during off-peak hours. Instead of fighting for a squat rack at 5:30 PM with everyone else, you can walk into a nearly empty gym at 6 AM, 10 PM, or 2 AM and have the entire floor to yourself.

2. Anytime Fitness — Best Franchise 24/7 Option

Multiple locations across Jacksonville

24/7 Access: Yes, all memberships

Typical price: $40-50/month

Anytime Fitness is the most recognizable 24/7 gym brand in Jacksonville, with multiple locations throughout the city. Every membership includes a key fob that works at any Anytime Fitness worldwide, which is a significant perk for travelers or people who move between neighborhoods.

The trade-off is space and equipment. Most Anytime Fitness Jacksonville locations are relatively small, with limited free weight areas. You'll usually find 1-2 squat racks, dumbbells up to 75-100 lbs, a handful of machines, and cardio equipment. For basic strength training and cardio, it works. For serious lifting, you'll outgrow it quickly.

Best for: Convenience, travelers, basic fitness

3. Planet Fitness — Budget 24/7 Access

Multiple locations across Jacksonville

24/7 Access: Yes, with Black Card membership ($24.99/month)

Basic price: $10/month (limited hours)

Planet Fitness offers 24/7 access at most Jacksonville locations for Black Card members. At $24.99/month, it's one of the more affordable 24/7 options. The facilities are clean and well-maintained, with plenty of cardio machines, a circuit area, and basic resistance equipment.

The catch: Planet Fitness doesn't carry free barbells at many locations, prohibits deadlifts, and has a "lunk alarm" policy. If your training involves compound barbell movements, heavy lifting, or anything that makes noise, Planet Fitness 24/7 access won't help you — because the equipment you need isn't there.

Best for: Late-night cardio, budget-conscious members who don't need free weights

4. Workout Anytime — Affordable 24/7 Chain

Multiple locations in the Jacksonville area

24/7 Access: Yes, all memberships

Typical price: $19-25/month

Workout Anytime is a smaller franchise chain with several Jacksonville locations. They offer 24/7 access at a competitive price point, with a mix of machines, free weights, and cardio in a compact space. The equipment is a step up from Planet Fitness — you'll find barbells and squat racks — but the facilities are small and the equipment inventory is limited.

Best for: Budget-friendly 24/7 access with basic free weights

5. Snap Fitness Jacksonville

24/7 Access: Yes

Typical price: $35-45/month

What to Look for in a 24/7 Gym

Not all 24-hour gyms are created equal. Here's what matters beyond just the access hours:

  • Security: Key fob entry, security cameras, well-lit parking. This is especially important if you train at 2 AM. Iron House has key fob entry and cameras throughout the facility.
  • Equipment availability: Some gyms lock certain equipment areas after hours or reduce lighting in parts of the facility. At Iron House, you have access to the entire 18,000+ sq ft facility 24/7.
  • Cleanliness: A gym that's only staffed 12 hours a day but open 24 needs to stay clean. Check reviews and visit during off-hours to see how the gym is maintained.
  • Community: Even at 2 AM, some gyms have a late-night crew of regulars. That sense of community can make a big difference in your motivation.
  • Location: A 24/7 gym that's 30 minutes away defeats the purpose. Pick the best gym within a reasonable drive. Iron House is conveniently located off Arlington Expressway, close to the Arlington, Southside, and Regency areas.

Who Needs a 24/7 Gym in Jacksonville?

  • Military personnel: Naval Station Mayport and NAS Jacksonville service members often have unpredictable schedules. Iron House supports the military community with flexible access.
  • Healthcare workers: Nurses, doctors, and techs at Baptist, Mayo, UF Health, and Memorial often work 12-hour shifts that end well after standard gym hours.
  • First responders: Firefighters and police officers on rotating shifts need a gym that works around their schedule, not the other way around.
  • Parents: Sometimes the only time to train is after the kids are asleep. A 24/7 gym means you can get your workout in at 9 PM, 10 PM, or later.
  • Early birds: If your alarm goes off at 4 AM and you want to lift before work, your gym needs to be open.
