Where Strength Meets Community

At Iron House Gym Jacksonville, our mission is to unite all strength sports under one roof, creating a supportive and motivating environment where athletes of every discipline can excel.

We cater to competitors — powerlifters, bodybuilders, Olympic lifters, strongmen — and to everyday people who just want to get healthier and stronger. Whether you're prepping for the stage or stepping into a gym for the first time, you'll find the equipment, coaching, and community you need to reach your goals.

What We Offer

From one-on-one coaching to recovery services, everything you need under one roof.

Personal Training

Customized, results-focused programs from our expert coaching staff. Whether your goal is strength, hypertrophy, or competition prep, we've got you covered.

Powerlifting

Competition-grade squat racks, benches, and deadlift platforms. Train with athletes who understand the sport and push each other to new PRs.

Bodybuilding

Full selection of machines, cables, and free weights for every muscle group. Posing area and prep coaching available for competitors.

Olympic Lifting

Dedicated platforms with bumper plates and competition barbells. Coaching available for snatch, clean & jerk technique development.

Strongman

Atlas stones, yoke, farmer's handles, logs, and more. Train for competition or just build raw, functional strength.

Massage Therapy

On-site licensed massage therapy for recovery and performance. Sessions from 30 minutes to 2 hours. Book by phone or text.
